About Me

#about, #longstoryshort

I hold an MA in Japanese Studies, which basically means that I can speak Japanese, and can work together with Japanese people fairy well, as I have a broad knowledge of the Japanese culture, traditional or popular, society, language etc.

That's how I started working at a small, family-owned Japanese business, where soon enough I became the assistant manager, then the sole in-house graphic designer, digital marketer and web designer.

With the very basics of HTML and CSS, which I learnt at a short course at the London Academy of IT, I started building the company's website. It took me around 1 month to finish this project, with long-long hours spent on W3Schools and StackOverflow to figure out how to make my code work, and on creating all the visual assets I wanted to feature on the site.

And I enjoyed every second of it, so I deep dived into web design & development...
